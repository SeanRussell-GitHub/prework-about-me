A Cramps fan page for fan people

From humble beginings in Akron, Ohio began the lives of
two of the greatest musicians to ever grace the stage...
Poison Ivy Rorshach and Lux Interior!
This duo created one of the most influential and unique bands of all time, The Cramps.

Some of their most popular songs are:

  • Human Fly
  • Garbage Man
  • Goo Goo Muck
  • I Was A Teenage Werewolf

Never heard of them? That is not surprising. They are the true unsung heroes of Rock'n Roll.
